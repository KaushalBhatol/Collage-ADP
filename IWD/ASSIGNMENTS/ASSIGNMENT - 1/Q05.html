My Favourite National Leader

There is no end to the choice of a favorite leader but as far as I am concerned, Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri is my favourite leader. He was born on October 2nd, 1904, at Mughalsarai, a town of UP. He belonged to a lower-middle-class family. He was born and brought up in the cradle of poverty

Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri got his early education at Kashi Vidyapeeth. It was the place where he absorbed the rich springs of Indian culture and civilization. He molded his living according to the Indian way of life. After completing his education, Shri Lal Bahadur Shastri turned to the poor condition of his countrymen under the British rule. He was deeply moved by the sufferings and miseries of his countrymen. Compelled by these circumstances, he joined the Indian freedom struggle. He came in contact with great Indian national leaders like Nehru, Gandhi, Patel and Subhas Chandra Bose. He worked hard and fought bravely in order to get his country liberated from the foreign rule.
